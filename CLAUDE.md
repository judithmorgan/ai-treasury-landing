# AI Treasury — Claude Code Project Notes

## About this business
AI Treasury is an AI-powered treasury and cash management platform
built for small-to-mid-sized businesses. It gives finance teams
real-time visibility into cash position, AI-driven forecasting, and
automated bank reconciliation, without needing a full treasury
department.

## Brand voice
- Clear, confident, and trustworthy — write like a knowledgeable advisor, not a corporate brand
- Use contractions ("you're," "it's," "let's")
- Short sentences over long compound ones
- Avoid financial jargon; explain any necessary term in plain language
- No fear-based or "you're doing it wrong" framing — lead with the benefit
